Teaching Philosophy

I have a vision for empowering youth to become world-class leaders. I believe children can achieve greatness through hard work and high expectations. I want to work alongside people who are equally driven, self-motivated and committed to closing the achievement gap.

As an artist and dancer, I believe that creativity and innovation is essential to inspiring youth. As a web developer, I am passionate about making technology accesible for all people.
